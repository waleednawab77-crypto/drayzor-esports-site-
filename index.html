Tryouts kaise honge (Step-by-step)

  1. Registration: Google Form se register karna hoga (IGN, mobile model, rank, K/D, MySocial links, availability).
  2. Screening: Organizers entries dekh ke shortlist karenge (rank, recent replays, behaviour).
  3. Warm-up & Rules Brief: Short meeting on live stream or Discord before matches.
  4. Tryout Matches: Short scrims / custom rooms. Har player ko multiple matches diya jayega. Observers record karenge.
  5. Evaluation Metrics: K/D, damage, positioning, communication, teamplay, attitude.
  6. Selection: Top performers ko invite/contract offer. Public announcement on website + DM.
  7. Schedule & Timing: Sab players ko time slot milega — website pe schedule update rahega.
Checklist for participants:
  • BGMI ID (IGN) and screenshot of rank
  • Stable internet (mobile data/Wi‑Fi)
  • Mic/headset for voice
  • No cheating; fair play required

Live Streaming — Free Options (quick)

Aap freely YouTube / Streamlabs Mobile / Omlet Arcade use kar sakte ho. Agar aap mobile se directly stream karna chahte ho to Streamlabs mobile app simple rahega. Agar aap PC se professional stream chahte ho to phone ko PC pe mirror karke OBS me capture karo (free tools maujood hain).

Option A — Direct mobile stream (easiest)
  1. Install Streamlabs (Android/iOS) — login to your YouTube/Twitch account.
  2. Use "Screen Share" within app to stream gameplay directly.
  3. Advantages: no PC, easy alerts/chat.
(Streamlabs mobile is free to use for single platform streaming.)
Option B — PC + OBS (more control)
  1. Mirror Android screen to PC using scrcpy (USB + enable developer options).
  2. Open scrcpy window, then in OBS add a "Window Capture" or "Game Capture" of the scrcpy window.
  3. Stream from OBS to YouTube (use stream key) — more overlays, scenes, webcam, and mic control.
(scrcpy is free and open-source; OBS is free.)
NOTE: YouTube has some eligibility rules for live streaming (verification, no strikes, and mobile streaming often needs a small subscriber minimum). Also YouTube raised the minimum streaming age recently — check your channel settings before planning a stream.
